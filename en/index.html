About me

I was once that mother, too —
longing to be truly held

I birthed both of my children gently — my first in Bremen, Germany, my second at home in Taiwan. Those two births, and years of raising children across Taiwan and Japan, taught me that becoming a mother is never only about the body. It moves everything — body, mind, and spirit.

Because I've walked it, I understand the longing not just for information, but to be genuinely understood. I weave evidence-based birth knowledge together with a body-mind-spirit approach, and walk beside each woman in her own way — steady, real, and never rushing you toward any "should."

— Milliya
